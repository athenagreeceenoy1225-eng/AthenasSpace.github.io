A dream I can't reach

As I have grown older, I started to realize that not all dreams are meant to come true. When I was younger, I believed that every dream I had could be reached if I just worked hard enough. But as time passed, I began to see that some dreams are too far, too heavy, or simply not meant for me. At first, this thought made me sad because I felt like I was giving up. But now, I understand that accepting this truth does not mean failure,it means growth.

Life is not always about chasing the biggest or brightest dream. Sometimes, it is about finding peace in the small things that make us happy. With this new understanding, I have found a different kind of determination. I no longer want to measure my worth by how close I get to impossible dreams. Instead, I want to pursue anything that brings me joy, no matter how simple or ordinary it may seem. Happiness, after all, is the greatest success anyone can have.

All I truly want is to be at ease wherever life may take me. I want to wake up each day with a calm heart, knowing that I am living a life that feels right for me. I hope that the future version of myself will look back and feel proud not because I achieved every dream, but because I chose to live with contentment. I hope she smiles, knowing that she followed what made her happy, even if it was not the path others expected.

Dreams may sometimes remain as dreams, but that does not mean life loses its meaning. What matters most is how we choose to live in the present, how we embrace the journey, and how we find joy in the places where we stand. My wish for myself is simple: to be happy, to be content, and to be at peace with whatever life gives me. If I can achieve that, then I will know that I have lived well.
